Help Center

Find Answers Fast

Everything you need to start scraping, troubleshoot issues, and export your data with ScrapyX.

Read Documentation Ask the Community Check System Status

Getting Started

  1. Create an account and log in.
  2. Go to “New Scrape” and enter a URL or a search query.
  3. Run the job and watch progress in the dashboard.
  4. Open “Results” to preview items and export CSV/JSON/XLSX.

Better Extraction

  • Structured Data First: We read JSON‑LD and app JSON for products and lists.
  • Smart Heuristics: We detect repeated cards, prices, ratings, and images.
  • AI Assist: On tough pages, AI helps cluster and extract cleaner fields.

Export Results

  • Open your job, click Export, choose CSV, JSON, or XLSX.
  • REST API is available for automation.

Troubleshooting

“Blocked / 403 / Captcha”

We try browser headers and JS rendering. If still blocked, try a different URL, slow down the frequency, or use a proxy service.

“No items found”

Make sure the page lists products or articles. Try a category page or search results instead of a protected detail page.

“LinkedIn / login wall”

Some sites restrict automated scraping. Use public pages or official APIs for reliable data.

Popular FAQs

Can I start with a search phrase?

Yes. Enter a phrase like “best headphones under 5000” and we pick a relevant page to scrape.

Will images and ratings be captured?

If available, we include images, ratings, and review counts alongside names, prices, and links.

How do I rerun a failed job?

Open the job and hit restart, or create a fresh job with updated settings.

Visit the documentation for detailed guides, ask the community for tips, or check system status if something looks off.

Your data, handled responsibly

This page explains what information ScrapyX collects, how we use it, and the choices you have.

What we collect

  • Account info (username, email)
  • Profile details you choose to share
  • Scrape jobs you create and their results
  • Basic logs (IP, user agent, page paths)

How we use data

  • Authenticate your account and secure access
  • Run scrape jobs and show results in the dashboard
  • Improve reliability and detect errors
  • Provide exports you request (CSV/JSON/XLSX)

Storage & retention

  • Data is stored in application databases
  • Scrape results are kept for your history and exports
  • You can delete jobs from your history

Cookies & third‑party services

ScrapyX may use cookies for session management and security. Optional integrations like search or AI helpers only run when you use those features and may contact their providers to fulfill your request.

Security

We apply reasonable safeguards and avoid storing sensitive secrets in plain text. Still, no online system can be guaranteed perfectly secure. Use strong passwords and keep your credentials private.

Your choices

  • Access and update: Edit your profile details at any time.
  • Delete jobs: Remove scrape jobs and their saved results from history.
  • Exports: Download data in common formats for your own storage.
